How to put Foundation 


1-Always start by applying just a little bit of 
            foundation,
-Add until you have just the right amount
        of coverage. 
2- Concentrate the foundation in the center of your 
    face where redness, blemishes tend to be more problematic.
Whether you’re using a foundation brush or your fingertips
            apply foundation in a stippling motion, 
            which means gently tapping it into your skin.
            Avoid any wiping or rubbing motions

How to put Concealer 


1-Use your fingers or a beauty sponge to apply.
2-Once you’ve applied your foundation, draw with
 the concealer pencil on the areas of your face that naturally
    catch the light like your cheekbones, the bridge of your 
nose, and your T-zone.
    Use a darker concealer pencil two shades darker than
 your skin tone to contour softer areas 
3. Hack your concealer for fuller-looking lips. 
            Once you’ve applied your foundation
 (but before you’ve applied your lipstick), 
            use a fine-tip lip brush to trace around your lips with 
concealer

How to put Blusher 


1-Oval faces :tend to have prominent cheekbones and forehead
             that is slightly wider than the chin. 
2-Heart shaped faces:have a wide forehead and high
  cheekbones,and the face tapers to a narrow chin
             3-Round faces:are as wide as they are long.
            4-Triangular :your chin will be narrow and pointed ,
             your high cheekbones will be your most prominent
              feature.

How to put contour 


1- Create a Base:After you apply your foundation and concealer,
 brush on a light layer of translucent powder to your face to 
create a clean base so the contour can go on smoothly
            2-Choose the Right Product and Brush
3. Map Out Your Face:When applying the contour itself, 
build pigment as you go and use your bone structure as a guide.
 Use circular motions to sweep color to the areas
 you want to look smaller

How to put Highlighter 


1-Apply highlighter to the inner corners of your eyes.
2- Sweep highlighter onto your brow bones:The areas just below 
your eyebrows will catch a lot of light
            3- Add a dab of highlighter just above your upper lip.
            4-Brush highlighter over the center of your chin.
Be careful not to apply too much highlighter to this area.
 You only need a light dusting.

How to put Mascara 


1- Don’t underestimate the power of a good eyelash curler!
 Curling your lashes, focusing on the ends and corners,
            gives your mascara a head start. 
            2- Apply the mascara from root to tip. Begin at the root
 of your upper lashes, wiggling the wand back and forth as you
 work your way up to capture each individual lash. 
            Blinking lashes on the mascara wand can also help
            volumise lashes Layer up to three coats of mascara 
Apply mascara to bottom lashes using the tip of wand to avoid 
    clumping.

How to put Lipstick 


1-Use a small amount of any lightweight concealer
 or foundation that matches your skin tone
 and apply it all over your lips. This acts as a primer
            2-Using a lip liner to outline your lips gives your lips
 a clear shape and definition and helps you apply
 the lipstick with more precision.
            3-start by applying the lipstick on the center of your 
upper lip, just below the Cupid’s bow. 
            4- Now swipe the lipstick across your upper lip from the
 center to both corners, till the entire lip is covered.

How to put Lipgloss 


1-Finish all other makeup before applying your lip gloss.
2- Add a clear moisturizer to make your lip gloss last longer 
Use a lip pencil to really bring out your lips.
            4-Apply lipstick before your gloss, if you desire.
Put on your lip gloss:Spread your lip gloss lightly
 toward the edges of the same lip.
            Press your bottom and top lips together to spread
 the gloss from one lip to the other.
